# Privacy Policy for Andika

**Effective Date:** December 17, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 17, 2025

## Introduction

Andika ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Andika mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point: Andika is a note-taking app that works entirely offline on your device. Your notes and personal data never leave your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your information to external servers.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use Andika, you may voluntarily provide:

- **Notes Content**: Text notes and checklists you create
- **App Preferences**: Theme settings, language preferences, view mode, and sort preferences
- **Labels**: Custom labels you create for organizing notes
- **App Lock PIN**: If you enable app lock, your PIN is stored locally

### 2. Information Automatically Stored

The App automatically stores:

- **Note Metadata**: Creation dates, modification dates, and color preferences
- **Backup Files**: When you create backups, they are stored locally on your device

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Identity**: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses
- **Location Data**: We do not track your GPS location
- **Contacts**: We do not access your phone's contact list
- **Browsing History**: We do not monitor your internet activity
- **Note Contents**: We do not read, analyze, or upload the contents of your notes
- **Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you use the app
- **Biometric Data**: Biometric authentication is handled by your device's secure system

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively on your device for:

1. **Note Management**: Creating, editing, organizing, and displaying your notes
2. **Search Functionality**: Searching through your notes locally
3. **Backup & Restore**: Creating and restoring local backups
4. **App Customization**: Remembering your preferences and settings
5. **App Security**: Verifying your PIN or biometric for app lock

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All data remains on your device** - nothing is uploaded to any server
- **Notes** are stored in a local SQLite database
- **App preferences** are stored in local shared preferences
- **Backups** are stored in your device's local storage
- **No cloud sync**: Your data is not uploaded to any cloud services
- **No accounts required**: No registration, login, or personal information needed

### Security Measures
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security
- **App Lock**: Optional PIN and biometric authentication
- **App Storage**: Notes are stored in the app's private storage area
- **No Network Transmission**: Your notes are never sent over the internet

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third parties.**

- **No Analytics Services**: We don't use Google Analytics or similar tracking
- **No Advertising**: We don't show ads or use ad tracking
- **No Third-Party Data Collection**: We don't share data with third-party services
- **No Data Sales**: We never sell your information

## Third-Party Services

### Share Functionality
When you share notes using Andika:
- Notes are shared through Android's built-in sharing system
- The receiving app may have its own privacy policies
- We do not control how shared notes are handled by other apps

### Biometric Authentication
When you use biometric unlock:
- Biometric data is handled by Android's secure biometric system
- We do not access or store your fingerprint or face data
- Authentication is performed locally by your device

## Permissions Required

The app requires the following permissions:

- **Storage Access**: To save and restore backup files
- **Biometric**: For optional fingerprint/face unlock (handled by device)

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access and Control
- **Full Access**: All your notes remain on your device under your control
- **Export**: Export notes via backup anytime
- **Delete**: Delete individual notes or all data at any time
- **Modify Settings**: Change app preferences anytime

### Permissions
- **Manage Permissions**: Control app permissions in Android settings
- **Revoke Access**: Disable permissions at any time (may limit functionality)

### Data Deletion
- **Delete Notes**: Delete individual notes from within the app
- **Empty Trash**: Permanently delete notes from trash
- **Clear App Data**: Use Android settings to clear all app data
- **Uninstall**: Remove the app to delete all notes and preferences

## Children's Privacy

Andika is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require registration or personal information to use.

## International Users

Andika can be used worldwide. The same privacy protections apply regardless of your location since all data remains on your device and no information is transmitted.
