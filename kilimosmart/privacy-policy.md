# Privacy Policy for KilimoSmart

**Effective Date:** January 8, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 8, 2025

## Introduction

KilimoSmart ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the KilimoSmart mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point: KilimoSmart is designed to work completely offline. All your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use KilimoSmart, you may voluntarily provide:

- **Profile Information**: Name, email address (optional), phone number (optional), and region
- **Farm Information**: Farm name, location, size, soil type, and water source
- **Crop Data**: Details about crops you plant, planting dates, harvest dates, and farming activities
- **Financial Records**: Income and expense transactions related to your farming activities
- **Photos**: Images of plants you scan for disease detection
- **Notes**: Any additional notes or comments you add to your records

### 2. Information Automatically Collected

The App automatically stores:

- **Disease Scan Results**: Analysis results from plant disease detection
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the app features (stored locally only)
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version (for app optimization)
- **App Performance Data**: Crash reports and error logs (stored locally only)

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Location Data**: We do not track your precise location using GPS
- **Personal Contacts**: We do not access your phone's contact list
- **Internet Activity**: We do not monitor your internet browsing or other app usage
- **Biometric Data**: We do not collect fingerprints, face scans, or other biometric information

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively on your device for:

1. **Providing App Services**: Managing your crops, tracking finances, and detecting plant diseases
2. **App Functionality**: Storing your preferences (language, settings) and maintaining your data
3. **Improving User Experience**: Optimizing app performance and features
4. **Data Export**: Allowing you to export your own data when requested

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All data is stored locally** on your device using encrypted SQLite databases
- **No internet connection required** for any app functionality
- **No data transmission** to external servers or third parties
- **Your control**: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app

### Security Measures
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
- **App Security**: Data is stored in the app's private storage area, inaccessible to other apps
- **No Account Required**: No usernames, passwords, or account creation needed

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third parties.** Since all data remains on your device:

- **No Third-Party Services**: We don't use analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud storage
- **No Data Transmission**: Your information never leaves your device
- **No Government Disclosure**: Since we don't have access to your data, we cannot disclose it to authorities
- **No Business Transfers**: In any business transaction, your data remains on your device

### Data Export by You

You can export your own data in the following formats:
- **CSV files** for financial records and crop data
- **PDF reports** for farming summaries
- **Image files** for disease scan photos

This exported data is under your complete control.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access and Control
- **View All Data**: Access all your information through the app interface
- **Modify Information**: Edit or update any information you've entered
- **Delete Specific Records**: Remove individual crops, transactions, or disease scans
- **Complete Data Deletion**: Uninstall the app to remove all data

### Language and Preferences
- **Language Choice**: Switch between Swahili and English at any time
- **App Settings**: Customize app behavior and appearance
- **Data Export Control**: Choose what data to export and when

### No Account Requirements
- **No Registration**: Use the app without creating accounts or providing email
- **No Passwords**: No login credentials needed
- **Anonymous Use**: Use the app completely anonymously if desired

## Children's Privacy

KilimoSmart is designed for farmers aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

## International Users

While KilimoSmart is primarily designed for Tanzanian farmers, users from other countries may use the app. The same privacy protections apply regardless of your location since all data remains on your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Services We Do NOT Use
- **Analytics Services**: No Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar services
- **Advertising Networks**: No ads or ad tracking
- **Cloud Storage**: No data uploaded to clouds or servers
- **Social Media Integration**: No Facebook, Twitter, or other social platform connections
- **Payment Processors**: No in-app purchases or payment processing

### Device Operating System
The app operates within the privacy frameworks of your device's operating system (Android or iOS). Please review your device's privacy settings for additional control.

## Data Retention

- **Local Storage**: Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **No Automatic Deletion**: We don't automatically delete your data
- **User Control**: You decide how long to keep your farming records
- **Backup Responsibility**: You are responsible for backing up data you want to keep
