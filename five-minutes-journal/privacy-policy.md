# Privacy Policy for Daily Reflect

**Effective Date:** December 30, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 30, 2025

## Introduction

Daily Reflect ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Daily Reflect mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point: Daily Reflect is a personal journaling app that stores your journal entries locally on your device. Your journal entries, thoughts, and personal reflections never leave your device unless you choose to export them.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use Daily Reflect, you may voluntarily provide:

- **Journal Entries**: Your daily journal entries, including gratitude lists, intentions, and reflections
- **Mood Data**: Your selected mood for each journal entry
- **App Preferences**: Theme settings (light/dark mode), notification preferences, writing timer settings
- **Security Preferences**: Passcode and biometric authentication settings (stored locally)

### 2. Information Automatically Stored

The App automatically stores on your device:

- **Journal Data**: Your created journal entries and timestamps
- **Settings**: Your app preferences and configurations
- **Streak Data**: Your journaling streak and milestone information
- **Notification Schedules**: Reminder times for daily journaling

### 3. Information Collected by Third Parties

Our app uses the following third-party services:

#### Google AdMob (Advertising)
- We display advertisements through Google AdMob
- AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
- For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)
- We use RevenueCat to manage premium subscriptions
- RevenueCat processes purchase information through Google Play/App Store
- Purchase data is handled according to [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

### 4. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Journal Content**: We do not read, access, or transmit your journal entries to any server
- **Personal Identity**: We do not require names, emails, or phone numbers to use the app
- **Location Data**: We do not track your GPS location
- **Contacts**: We do not access your phone's contact list
- **Photos or Media**: We do not access your photo library

## How We Use Your Information

Your journal entries are used exclusively on your device for:

1. **Journaling**: Creating, editing, and organizing your journal entries
2. **Reminders**: Sending local notifications for daily journaling
3. **Statistics**: Showing your journaling streaks and milestones
4. **Personalization**: Remembering your theme and preferences
5. **Security**: Protecting your journal with passcode/biometric lock

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All journal entries remain on your device** - nothing is uploaded to any server
- **Entries** are stored in the app's local database
- **App preferences** are stored in local app storage
- **No cloud sync**: Your entries are not uploaded to any cloud services
- **No accounts required**: No registration or login needed

### Security Measures
- **Passcode Protection**: Optional app lock with passcode
- **Biometric Authentication**: Optional Face ID/fingerprint protection
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security
- **App Storage**: Entries are stored in the app's private storage area

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your journal entries to any third parties.**

Your journal entries are:
- Never transmitted over the internet
- Never stored on external servers
- Never shared with advertisers
- Never sold to data brokers

## Advertising

Daily Reflect displays advertisements to support free features:

- **Ad Provider**: Google AdMob
- **Ad Types**: Banner ads and occasional interstitial ads
- **Personalized Ads**: May be shown based on your Google ad settings
- **Opt-Out**: You can disable personalized ads in your device's Google settings
- **Premium Option**: Upgrade to premium to remove all ads

## In-App Purchases

We offer premium subscriptions through:

- **Monthly Subscription**: Removes ads and unlocks premium features
- **Yearly Subscription**: Same benefits with annual billing
- **Lifetime Purchase**: One-time purchase for permanent premium access

Purchases are processed by Google Play Store and managed through RevenueCat.

## Permissions Required

The app requires the following permissions:

- **Notifications**: To send daily journaling reminders
- **Biometric/Fingerprint**: For optional app lock feature
- **Vibration**: For haptic feedback when interacting with the app
- **Internet**: Required for displaying ads and processing purchases

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access and Control
- **Full Access**: All your journal entries remain on your device
- **Export**: Export your entries as text files anytime
- **Delete**: Delete individual entries or all data at any time
- **Modify Settings**: Change app preferences anytime

### Advertising Choices
- **Personalized Ads**: Opt out via your device's Google settings
- **Remove Ads**: Upgrade to premium for an ad-free experience

### Data Deletion
- **Clear All Data**: Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings
- **Uninstall**: Remove the app to delete all entries and preferences

## Children's Privacy

Daily Reflect is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require registration or personal information to use.

## International Users

Daily Reflect can be used worldwide. Your journal entries remain on your device regardless of your location.
