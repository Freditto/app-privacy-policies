# Delete Your Data - Five Minutes Journal

## How to Delete Your Data

All your journal entries and app data are stored **locally on your device**. You have complete control over your data.

### Delete All Data (In-App)

1. Open **Five Minutes Journal**
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Scroll to **Data & Storage**
4. Tap **Clear All Data**
5. Confirm the deletion

### What Gets Deleted

When you clear all data, the following is permanently removed:

- ✅ All journal entries
- ✅ Mood history
- ✅ Streak data and milestones
- ✅ Custom prompts
- ✅ Notification preferences
- ✅ Theme and display settings
- ✅ Passcode settings

### What Is NOT Stored

Five Minutes Journal does **not** store any data on external servers. Your journal entries never leave your device.

### Alternative: Uninstall the App

You can also delete all data by uninstalling the app from your device:

**Android:**
1. Long-press the Five Minutes Journal app icon
2. Select "Uninstall" or drag to "Uninstall"
3. Confirm the uninstall

This will remove all app data from your device.

### Third-Party Data

**Google AdMob**: If you've interacted with ads, Google may have collected advertising data. You can manage this at [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

**RevenueCat (Purchases)**: If you made a purchase, transaction records are maintained by Google Play and RevenueCat for billing purposes. Contact Google Play support for purchase-related data.
