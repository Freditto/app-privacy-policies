# Privacy Policy for EcoPull

**Effective Date:** November 28, 2025
**Last Updated:** November 28, 2025

## Introduction

EcoPull ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the EcoPull mobile game (the "Game").

**Key Point: EcoPull is designed to work completely offline. All your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information Stored Locally

When you play EcoPull, the following information is stored locally on your device:

- **Game Progress**: Level completion status, stars earned, and high scores
- **Game Settings**: Language preference (Swahili/English), sound settings, vibration settings
- **Game Statistics**: Questions answered, correct answers, accuracy percentage

### 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers
- **Location Data**: We do not track your location
- **Device Identifiers**: We do not collect device IDs or advertising identifiers
- **Internet Activity**: We do not monitor your internet usage
- **Contacts**: We do not access your contact list
- **Photos/Media**: We do not access your photos or media files
- **Biometric Data**: We do not collect fingerprints or face data

## How We Use Your Information

Your game data is used exclusively on your device for:

1. **Saving Progress**: Remembering your completed levels and scores
2. **Game Settings**: Storing your language and sound preferences
3. **Statistics Display**: Showing your game performance

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All data is stored locally** on your device
- **No internet connection required** for any game functionality
- **No data transmission** to external servers or third parties
- **Your control**: You can delete all data by uninstalling the game or resetting progress in settings

### Security Measures
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security
- **App-Private Storage**: Data is stored in the game's private storage, inaccessible to other apps
- **No Account Required**: No registration, usernames, or passwords needed

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties.**

Since all data remains on your device:
- **No Third-Party Services**: No analytics, advertising, or cloud services
- **No Data Transmission**: Your information never leaves your device
- **No Server Storage**: We don't operate servers that store user data

## Third-Party Services

### Services We Do NOT Use
- ❌ Analytics Services (No Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- ❌ Advertising Networks (No ads)
- ❌ Cloud Storage (No data uploads)
- ❌ Social Media Integration
- ❌ In-App Purchases or Payment Processing

## Children's Privacy

EcoPull is an educational game suitable for all ages, including children.

- **No Personal Data Collection**: We don't collect any personal information from users of any age
- **Safe for Children**: The game is designed to be safe for children aged 7 and above
- **Educational Content**: All content is family-friendly and educational
- **COPPA Compliance**: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

- **Reset Progress**: Clear all game progress from settings
- **Change Language**: Switch between Swahili and English anytime
- **Adjust Settings**: Modify sound and vibration preferences
- **Delete All Data**: Uninstall the game to remove all data

## Permissions

EcoPull requests minimal permissions:

- **Vibration**: For haptic feedback during gameplay (optional, can be disabled)
- **No Internet Permission**: The game works completely offline
- **No Storage Permission**: Uses only app-private storage
