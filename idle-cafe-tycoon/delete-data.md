# Delete Your Data - Idle Cafe Tycoon

## How to Delete Your Data

All your game progress and app data are stored **locally on your device**. You have complete control over your data.

### Reset Game Progress (In-App)

1. Open **Idle Cafe Tycoon**
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Scroll to **Reset Progress**
4. Tap **Reset Game**
5. Confirm the reset

### What Gets Deleted

When you reset your game, the following is permanently removed:

- All cafe progress and levels
- Money and gems balance
- Staff and equipment upgrades
- Achievements and milestones
- Game statistics
- All unlocked locations
- Notification preferences
- Sound and music settings

### What Is NOT Stored Externally

Idle Cafe Tycoon does **not** store any data on external servers. Your game progress never leaves your device.

### Alternative: Uninstall the App

You can also delete all data by uninstalling the app from your device:

**Android:**
1. Long-press the Idle Cafe Tycoon app icon
2. Select "Uninstall" or drag to "Uninstall"
3. Confirm the uninstall

This will remove all app data from your device.

### Third-Party Data

**Google AdMob**: If you've interacted with ads, Google may have collected advertising data. You can manage this at [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

**RevenueCat (Purchases)**: If you made a purchase, transaction records are maintained by Google Play and RevenueCat for billing purposes. Contact Google Play support for purchase-related data.
