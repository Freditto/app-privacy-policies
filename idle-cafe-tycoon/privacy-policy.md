# Privacy Policy for Idle Cafe Tycoon

**Effective Date:** January 4, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 4, 2026

## Introduction

Idle Cafe Tycoon ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Idle Cafe Tycoon mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point: Idle Cafe Tycoon is an idle tycoon game that stores your game progress locally on your device. Your game data remains on your device.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information Stored Locally

When you play Idle Cafe Tycoon, the following data is stored on your device:

- **Game Progress**: Your cafe level, money, gems, staff, equipment, and upgrades
- **Game Statistics**: Play time, achievements, and milestones
- **Settings**: Sound, music, notification preferences
- **Offline Earnings**: Time-based calculations for offline progress

### 2. Information Collected by Third Parties

Our app uses the following third-party services:

#### Google AdMob (Advertising)
- We display advertisements through Google AdMob
- AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization
- Ad types include: banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
- For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)
- We use RevenueCat to manage premium subscriptions
- RevenueCat processes purchase information through Google Play/App Store
- Purchase data is handled according to [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Identity**: We do not require names, emails, or phone numbers
- **Location Data**: We do not track your GPS location
- **Contacts**: We do not access your phone's contact list
- **Photos or Media**: We do not access your photo library
- **Game Data Transmission**: We do not upload your game progress to any server

## How We Use Your Information

Your game data is used exclusively on your device for:

1. **Gameplay**: Saving and loading your game progress
2. **Offline Earnings**: Calculating earnings while you're away
3. **Notifications**: Sending optional reminders about your cafe
4. **Personalization**: Remembering your settings and preferences

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All game data remains on your device**
- **No cloud sync**: Your progress is not uploaded to any cloud services
- **No accounts required**: No registration or login needed

### Security Measures
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security
- **App Storage**: Game data is stored in the app's private storage area

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your game data to any third parties.**

Your game progress is:
- Never transmitted over the internet
- Never stored on external servers
- Never shared with advertisers
- Never sold to data brokers

## Advertising

Idle Cafe Tycoon displays advertisements to support free gameplay:

- **Ad Provider**: Google AdMob
- **Ad Types**: Banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads
- **Rewarded Ads**: Watch ads voluntarily to earn bonus rewards
- **Personalized Ads**: May be shown based on your Google ad settings
- **Opt-Out**: You can disable personalized ads in your device's Google settings
- **Premium Option**: Upgrade to premium to remove all ads

## In-App Purchases

We offer premium options including:

- **Monthly Subscription**: Removes ads and unlocks premium benefits
- **Yearly Subscription**: Same benefits with annual billing
- **Lifetime Purchase**: One-time purchase for permanent premium access

Premium benefits include:
- Ad-free experience
- 2x earnings multiplier
- Exclusive rewards
- Unlimited boosts

Purchases are processed by Google Play Store and managed through RevenueCat.

## Permissions Required

The app requires the following permissions:

- **Internet**: Required for displaying ads and processing purchases
- **Notifications**: To send optional game reminders
- **Vibration**: For haptic feedback during gameplay

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access and Control
- **Full Access**: All your game data remains on your device
- **Reset Progress**: Reset your game anytime from Settings
- **Modify Settings**: Change app preferences anytime

### Advertising Choices
- **Personalized Ads**: Opt out via your device's Google settings
- **Remove Ads**: Upgrade to premium for an ad-free experience

### Data Deletion
- **Reset Game**: Use the "Reset Progress" option in Settings
- **Uninstall**: Remove the app to delete all game data

## Children's Privacy

## International Users
