# Privacy Policy for Chakula Calculator

**Effective Date:** November 14, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025

## Introduction

Chakula Calculator ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Chakula Calculator mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point: Chakula Calculator is designed to work completely offline. All your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use Chakula Calculator, you may voluntarily provide:

- **Calculation Data**: Food portion calculations you perform, guest counts, and meal planning data
- **Saved Calculations**: Calculation results you choose to save for future reference
- **Language Preference**: Choice between English and Swahili
- **App Settings**: Display preferences and measurement unit settings
- **User Notes**: Any additional notes you add to saved calculations

### 2. Information Automatically Collected

The App automatically stores:

- **Usage Data**: Information about which features you use most (stored locally only)
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version (for app optimization)
- **App Performance Data**: Crash reports and error logs (stored locally only)
- **Calculation History**: Record of food portion calculations performed

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Identity**: We do not require or collect names, emails, or phone numbers
- **Location Data**: We do not track your precise location using GPS
- **Payment Information**: We do not collect any financial or payment data
- **Internet Activity**: We do not monitor your internet browsing or other app usage
- **Device Contacts**: We do not access your phone's contact list
- **Camera/Photos**: We do not access your device's camera or photo library
- **Microphone**: We do not access or record audio

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively on your device for:

1. **Providing Calculation Services**: Food portion calculations for Tanzanian cuisine
2. **Saving Preferences**: Remembering your language choice and app settings
3. **Calculation History**: Storing your saved calculations for future reference
4. **Cultural Education**: Providing information about traditional Tanzanian foods and measurements
5. **App Functionality**: Enabling offline food portion planning and sharing features

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All data is stored locally** on your device using SharedPreferences
- **No internet connection required** for core app functionality
- **No data transmission** to external servers or third parties
- **Your control**: You can delete all data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app

### Security Measures
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
- **App Security**: Data is stored in the app's private storage area, inaccessible to other apps
- **No Account Required**: No usernames, passwords, or account creation needed
- **Local Encryption**: Data is stored securely using Android/iOS security frameworks

### Sharing Features
- **PDF Generation**: PDF files are created locally on your device for sharing
- **Text Sharing**: Calculation results can be shared as text through your device's sharing system
- **No Cloud Storage**: Shared content is generated locally and sent through your chosen apps
- **No Data Tracking**: We do not track what you share or with whom

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third parties.** Since all data remains on your device:

- **No Third-Party Services**: We don't use analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud storage
- **No Data Transmission**: Your calculation data never leaves your device
- **No Business Sharing**: We cannot share data with restaurants, catering businesses, or food services
- **No Government Disclosure**: We cannot disclose data to authorities since it remains on your device
- **No Business Transfers**: In any business transaction, your data remains on your device

### User-Initiated Sharing
- **Manual Sharing Only**: Content is only shared when you explicitly choose to share it
- **Device Sharing**: Sharing uses your device's built-in sharing capabilities
- **No Automatic Sharing**: We never automatically share your calculations or data
- **No Social Media Integration**: No automatic posting to social media platforms

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your calculation data:

### Access and Control
- **View All Data**: Access all your saved calculations through the app interface
- **Modify Information**: Edit or update any saved calculations
- **Delete Specific Records**: Remove individual calculations from your history
- **Complete Data Deletion**: Clear all app data through device settings or uninstall the app

### Language and Preferences
- **Language Choice**: Switch between Swahili and English at any time
- **App Settings**: Customize display options and measurement preferences
- **Feature Control**: Choose which app features to use

### No Account Requirements
- **No Registration**: Use the app without creating accounts or providing personal information
- **No Passwords**: No login credentials needed
- **Anonymous Use**: Use the app completely anonymously

## Children's Privacy

Chakula Calculator contains food and cultural information suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app can be safely used by children with parental guidance for learning about food planning and Tanzanian culture.

## International Users

While Chakula Calculator is primarily designed for users in Tanzania and East Africa, users from other countries may use the app to learn about Tanzanian cuisine. The same privacy protections apply regardless of your location since all data remains on your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Services We Do NOT Use
- **Analytics Services**: No Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar services
- **Advertising Networks**: No ads or ad tracking
- **Cloud Storage**: No calculation data uploaded to clouds or servers
- **Social Media Integration**: No Facebook, Twitter, or other social platform connections
- **Payment Processors**: No in-app purchases or payment processing
- **Food Delivery Services**: No connections to food delivery or restaurant platforms

### Device Operating System
The app operates within the privacy frameworks of your device's operating system (Android or iOS). Please review your device's privacy settings for additional control over app permissions.

## Cultural and Educational Disclaimer

### Educational Purpose
- **Cultural Information**: Food information is for educational and planning purposes only
- **Traditional Measurements**: Traditional measurements are approximations based on cultural practices
- **No Dietary Advice**: The app does not provide nutritional or dietary advice
- **Professional Consultation**: Consult nutrition professionals for specific dietary needs

## Data Retention

- **Local Storage**: Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **No Automatic Deletion**: We don't automatically delete your saved calculations
- **User Control**: You decide how long to keep your calculation history
- **Backup Responsibility**: You are responsible for backing up data you want to preserve
