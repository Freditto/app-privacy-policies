# Privacy Policy for Afya Salama

**Effective Date:** November 8, 2024  
**Last Updated:** November 8, 2024

## Introduction

Afya Salama ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Afya Salama mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point: Afya Salama is designed to work completely offline. All your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use Afya Salama, you may voluntarily provide:

- **Health Information**: Symptoms you record, health conditions you track, and medical notes
- **Personal Health Records**: Vital signs, medications, and health history you choose to store
- **Emergency Contacts**: Contact information for emergency purposes (stored locally)
- **Language Preference**: Choice between English and Swahili
- **App Settings**: Notification preferences and display settings
- **User Notes**: Any additional health-related notes or reminders you add

### 2. Information Automatically Collected

The App automatically stores:

- **Usage Data**: Information about which features you use most (stored locally only)
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version (for app optimization)
- **App Performance Data**: Crash reports and error logs (stored locally only)
- **Feature Access History**: Record of first aid procedures accessed and symptom checks performed

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Identity**: We do not require or collect names, emails, or phone numbers
- **Location Data**: We do not track your precise location using GPS
- **Biometric Data**: We do not collect fingerprints, face scans, or other biometric information
- **Internet Activity**: We do not monitor your internet browsing or other app usage
- **Device Contacts**: We do not access your phone's contact list
- **Camera/Photos**: We do not access your device's camera or photo library

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively on your device for:

1. **Providing Health Services**: Symptom checking, first aid guidance, and health tracking
2. **Emergency Response**: Enabling quick access to emergency procedures and contacts
3. **Personalized Experience**: Remembering your language preference and app settings
4. **Health Education**: Providing relevant medical information and first aid procedures
5. **App Functionality**: Storing your health records and medical notes securely on your device

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All data is stored locally** on your device using encrypted SQLite databases
- **No internet connection required** for core app functionality
- **No data transmission** to external servers or third parties
- **Your control**: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app

### Security Measures
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
- **App Security**: Data is stored in the app's private storage area, inaccessible to other apps
- **Encryption**: All health data is stored using industry-standard encryption
- **No Account Required**: No usernames, passwords, or account creation needed

### Emergency Features
- **Emergency Calls**: The app can initiate emergency calls using your device's dialer
- **Call Permissions**: We only use call permissions for emergency situations you initiate
- **No Call Recording**: We do not record, monitor, or store any call information

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third parties.** Since all data remains on your device:

- **No Third-Party Services**: We don't use analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud storage
- **No Data Transmission**: Your health information never leaves your device
- **No Medical Disclosure**: Since we don't have access to your data, we cannot share it with healthcare providers
- **No Government Disclosure**: We cannot disclose data to authorities since it remains on your device
- **No Business Transfers**: In any business transaction, your data remains on your device

### Emergency Calls
- **User-Initiated Only**: Emergency calls are only made when you explicitly request them
- **Direct Dialing**: Calls go directly through your device's phone system
- **No Call Monitoring**: We do not monitor, record, or have access to your emergency calls

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your health data:

### Access and Control
- **View All Data**: Access all your information through the app interface
- **Modify Information**: Edit or update any health records you've entered
- **Delete Specific Records**: Remove individual health entries, symptoms, or notes
- **Complete Data Deletion**: Uninstall the app to remove all data permanently

### Language and Preferences
- **Language Choice**: Switch between Swahili and English at any time
- **App Settings**: Customize notifications, display options, and emergency contacts
- **Feature Control**: Choose which app features to use

### No Account Requirements
- **No Registration**: Use the app without creating accounts or providing personal information
- **No Passwords**: No login credentials needed
- **Anonymous Use**: Use the app completely anonymously

## Children's Privacy

Afya Salama contains medical information and emergency procedures suitable for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please use this app with parental guidance. Parents and guardians are responsible for monitoring their children's use of the app.

## International Users

While Afya Salama is primarily designed for users in Kenya and East Africa, users from other countries may use the app. The same privacy protections apply regardless of your location since all data remains on your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Services We Do NOT Use
- **Analytics Services**: No Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar services
- **Advertising Networks**: No ads or ad tracking
- **Cloud Storage**: No health data uploaded to clouds or servers
- **Social Media Integration**: No Facebook, Twitter, or other social platform connections
- **Payment Processors**: No in-app purchases or payment processing
- **Medical Networks**: No connections to hospitals, clinics, or medical record systems

### Device Operating System
The app operates within the privacy frameworks of your device's operating system (Android or iOS). Please review your device's privacy settings for additional control over app permissions.

## Medical Disclaimer

### Not a Substitute for Professional Medical Care
- **Educational Purpose**: Information provided is for educational purposes only
- **Seek Professional Help**: Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical advice
- **Emergency Situations**: In real emergencies, call professional emergency services immediately
- **No Medical Diagnosis**: The app does not provide medical diagnoses or replace professional medical judgment

## Data Retention

- **Local Storage**: Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **No Automatic Deletion**: We don't automatically delete your health records
- **User Control**: You decide how long to keep your health information
- **Backup Responsibility**: You are responsible for backing up data you want to preserve
