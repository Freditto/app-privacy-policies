# Privacy Policy for Vocito

**Effective Date:** December 17, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 17, 2025

## Introduction

Vocito ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Vocito mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point: Vocito is a voice recorder that works entirely offline on your device. Your voice recordings and personal data never leave your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your information to external servers.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use Vocito, you may voluntarily provide:

- **App Preferences**: Theme settings, audio quality preferences, and other settings
- **Recording Names**: Titles you assign to your voice recordings
- **Recording Organization**: Custom organization of your recordings

### 2. Information Automatically Accessed

The App automatically accesses:

- **Microphone**: To capture voice recordings
- **Storage**: To save voice recordings locally on your device
- **File Metadata**: Recording names, durations, sizes, and creation dates

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Identity**: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses
- **Location Data**: We do not track your GPS location
- **Contacts**: We do not access your phone's contact list
- **Browsing History**: We do not monitor your internet activity
- **Recording Contents**: We do not listen to, analyze, or upload the contents of your voice recordings
- **Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you use the app
- **Biometric Data**: We do not collect fingerprint or face data

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively on your device for:

1. **Voice Recording**: Capturing and processing audio from your microphone
2. **Audio Playback**: Playing back your saved recordings
3. **Recording Organization**: Managing and organizing your recordings
4. **App Features**: Remembering your preferences and settings

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All data remains on your device** - nothing is uploaded to any server
- **Voice recordings** are stored in the app's local storage
- **App preferences** are stored in local app storage
- **No cloud sync**: Your data is not uploaded to any cloud services
- **No accounts required**: No registration, login, or personal information needed

### Security Measures
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security
- **App Storage**: Recordings are stored in the app's private storage area
- **No Network Transmission**: Your recordings are never sent over the internet

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third parties.**

- **No Analytics Services**: We don't use Google Analytics or similar tracking
- **No Advertising**: We don't show ads or use ad tracking
- **No Third-Party Data Collection**: We don't share data with third-party services
- **No Data Sales**: We never sell your information

## Third-Party Services

### Share Functionality
When you share recordings using Vocito:
- Recordings are shared through Android's built-in sharing system
- The receiving app may have its own privacy policies
- We do not control how shared recordings are handled by other apps

## Permissions Required

The app requires the following permissions:

- **Microphone**: To record voice audio
- **Storage/Media Access**: To save voice recordings locally
- **Vibrate**: For haptic feedback during recording
- **Internet** (optional): Only for links to external pages; not required for core recording functionality

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access and Control
- **Full Access**: All your recordings remain on your device under your control
- **Export**: Share or export recordings anytime
- **Delete**: Delete individual recordings or all data at any time
- **Modify Settings**: Change app preferences anytime

### Permissions
- **Manage Permissions**: Control app permissions in Android settings
- **Revoke Access**: Disable permissions at any time (may limit functionality)

### Data Deletion
- **Clear App Data**: Use Android settings to clear all app data
- **Uninstall**: Remove the app to delete all recordings and preferences

## Children's Privacy

Vocito is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require registration or personal information to use.

## International Users

Vocito can be used worldwide. The same privacy protections apply regardless of your location since all data remains on your device and no information is transmitted.
