# Privacy Policy for Fileora

**Effective Date:** December 17, 2024
**Last Updated:** December 17, 2024

## Introduction

Fileora ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Fileora mobile application (the "App").

**Key Point: Fileora is a file manager that works entirely offline on your device. Your files and personal data never leave your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your information.**

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use Fileora, you may voluntarily provide:

- **App Preferences**: Theme settings (light/dark mode), view preferences, and other settings
- **Favorites**: Files and folders you mark as favorites

### 2. Information Automatically Accessed

The App automatically accesses:

- **File System**: Files and folders stored on your device (for browsing and management)
- **Storage Information**: Available and used storage space (for storage analysis features)
- **File Metadata**: File names, sizes, types, and modification dates

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Personal Identity**: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses
- **Location Data**: We do not track your GPS location
- **Contacts**: We do not access your phone's contact list
- **Browsing History**: We do not monitor your internet activity
- **File Contents**: We do not read, analyze, or upload the contents of your files
- **Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you use the app

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively on your device for:

1. **File Management**: Browsing, copying, moving, renaming, and deleting files
2. **Storage Analysis**: Showing storage breakdown by file category
3. **File Preview**: Displaying thumbnails and previews of images and videos
4. **App Features**: Managing favorites and remembering your preferences

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
- **All data remains on your device** - nothing is uploaded to any server
- **App preferences** are stored in local app storage
- **No cloud sync**: Your data is not uploaded to any cloud services
- **No accounts required**: No registration, login, or personal information needed

### Security Measures
- **Device Security**: Data is protected by your device's built-in security
- **App Storage**: Preferences are stored in the app's private storage area
- **No Network Access**: The app works entirely offline

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third parties.**

- **No Analytics Services**: We don't use Google Analytics or similar tracking
- **No Advertising**: We don't show ads or use ad tracking
- **No Third-Party SDKs**: We don't share data with third-party services
- **No Data Sales**: We never sell your information

## Third-Party Services

### Share Functionality
When you share files using Fileora:
- Files are shared through Android's built-in sharing system
- The receiving app may have its own privacy policies
- We do not control how shared files are handled by other apps

### Open Files
When you open files with external apps:
- Files are opened through Android's default app handlers
- The app handling the file may have its own privacy policies

## Permissions Required

The app requires the following permissions:

- **Storage/Media Access**: To read and manage your files
- **Manage External Storage**: To access all files on your device (Android 11+)

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access and Control
- **Full Access**: All your files remain on your device under your control
- **Modify Settings**: Change app preferences anytime
- **Clear Data**: Use Android settings to clear app data
- **Uninstall**: Remove the app to delete all app preferences

### Permissions
- **Manage Permissions**: Control app permissions in Android settings
- **Revoke Access**: Disable permissions at any time (may limit functionality)

## Children's Privacy

Fileora is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require registration or personal information to use.

## International Users

Fileora can be used worldwide. The same privacy protections apply regardless of your location since all data remains on your device and no information is transmitted.
